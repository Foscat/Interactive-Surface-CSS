# Code of Conduct

## Commitment

This project is committed to a respectful, professional, harassment-free environment for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of expected behavior:

- be respectful and constructive
- focus on technical issues, not people
- accept feedback professionally
- assume good intent where reasonable
- keep discussions clear and actionable

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- personal attacks or insults
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- repeated bad-faith or deliberately disruptive conduct
- sexualized language or unwelcome sexual attention

## Scope

This policy applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and project-related communication.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action for violations, including:

- warning
- comment or content removal
- temporary participation restrictions
- permanent bans from project spaces

## Reporting

Report violations privately to:

`https://github.com/Foscat`

If needed, open a minimal issue requesting private follow-up from the maintainer.

## Project Standard

Direct technical disagreement is acceptable. Personal hostility is not.
